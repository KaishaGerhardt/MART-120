TIME

Watching the clock is a waste of time
I continuously watch the clock take time away
It sometimes feels like forever or now recently, too fast
Wishing that things would go by fast is something that should not be wished for
For pain, yes
For growing up, no
I’m eager to grow and leave but it scares me how fast it happened
It seems that I was just a little girl wanting to run away a few days ago
Now I'm an adult still wanting to run away, but for different reasons now
Just looking back, it's strange to think I've lived this long
I felt that I would have died a long time ago
But no
I'm surprisingly still here
And still watching the clock
Tick and ticking away
Minutes
Hours
Days
Months
Years
Of my life.

The Thoughts From Her


The Thoughts From Her
There she is, the not yet bloomed tree in a forest full of tall strong cherry blossom trees
The feeling of insecurity, as a red rabbit feels the endangerment of standing in a snowy plane….alone.
But she tricks her mind as a hypnotist tricks his clients
To believe she is a dictator of confidence.
She starts to express many thoughts
From that she works off, working in her mind day and night with only in a few minutes
She has waterfalls of thoughts
...some too harsh and some too soft to speak…
She turns to the one thought she knows all too well …..Herself…..
Yet she is a stranger at the same
She writes as a typewriter types, collecting what to produce next.
Therefore time slips from the clock wiping it clean of the past.
She stands as the unblossomed tree
Prepares to speak, the birds in her mind chirp
So here I am speaking my chirp before you
All blossomed cherry trees
